Scouts Summer Camp 2022

   What does it mean to be actively involved in your local community? How can you grow as a person,
push yourself out of your comfort zone and improve your skill set all at the same time?
   If your age ranges between 11 and 18, then join us for 2 weeks in nature, in our annual
Summer Camp, for a chance to rediscover yourself and the world around you.

Who are we?

Our local scouts center was founded in 2013 by a handful of people who wanted to make a change for the youth in Drobeta Turnu-Severin, and offer them the possibility of a non-formal education. Now our numbers have grown up to approximately 150 active members and we are proud to present a strong community of both children and adults, working together for a better future.

More details about the Summer Camp

This year's edition will take place between the 25th and 31st of August, in a location near Băile Herculane. For a week, kids are invited to sleep in tents, cook for themselves, gather wood and provide clean water for the whole group. A normal day in camp consists of 3 big meals and 2 snacks, daily duties (cooking, cleaning, collecting and chopping down wood and collecting clean water),workshops and leisure time. Everyone in camp will experience each of the daily duties in turns. The workshops are provided by our leaders, educated in many fields such as Sustainable Development, Foreign Languages, Media Communication and many more. Further details about camping activities and costs you can find on our social media or in the Google Forms you can submit to register as a participant. All files necessary for attendance and parental agreement forms can also be found on our Facebook page. 

It is not a prerequisite to already be a scout to join this event. This camp is open to anyone aged 11 to 18 who might be interested in our activities. It is a great opportunity for anyone to get a feel of what we do in our organization and what we generally offer for our youth. 

What we offer

Practical skills

Every year we offer teens the possibility to learn how to light a campfire, how to gather and safely chop down wood, find clean water, cook for themselves, and how to safely do all of these tasks, among many other things.

Interpersonal skills

Even if you're new to scouting, you have the opportunity to make many new friends and  improve your team work, multitasking, analyzing and creative problem solving abilities through a wide range of fun activities.

Connect with nature

Scouting is also about learning how to best take care of the world surrounding us. Through talking with local experts and first hand experiences, you can learn more about aligning yourself to a more sustainable lifestyle.

Make a change

Every year, our team aims to leave a positive mark on the spots we visit. Canoeing and hiking is fun, but once you combine that with collecting trash out of the water and up the trail, you can be certain that you leave every place better than you found it.
